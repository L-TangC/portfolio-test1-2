Concept

An instrument store's wedsite of mine. Customers can browse the web to get the informations about this store.
This website have different funtions, like Blogs, teacher informations and online stores.

Ideate

I thought the importants to have an online store for every saling companies after I closed my instrument store. As a entry UI designer, I decided to build this website for practice.
I list up all the work that I done at the store, and choose the most important part that could benefit the online users.
Online store can convenient customers to buy online. Blog is a good way to increase trusts with customers and also interact with them.

Research

It's important that customers to find what they want in the website, so the menu have a very clear categoray for users to search. I choose "Product pade" set on the home-page, it can get the customers a strong impression as, we have sale products online. Not only a corporate image wedsite.
The second part that we focus on is the "Instrument Courses" for those customers who what to learn ukulele or guitar. We put the lesson banner on each pages, and push the users to browse other Blogs to know us better, it's a good way to get the trust from customers.
