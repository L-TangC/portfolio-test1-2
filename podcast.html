Concept

"Tang Breaks Silence" is a podcast of mine, started at September 14th,2020. Now have over 40 episodes of the show. This is a interview show that I chat with different person on each jobs.
Those multiple interviewees that I chat with, have there own uniqe stores. It not only a great exprience for me, but also for every listeners.

Ideate

The reason that I made this podcast is, Everyone have their own life, and only roll once at the time. It's a good chance to share their own career with others, so everyone can listen vast of lifestyles.
The idea was came from the work of my instrument store. I met plenty of customers everyday, made lots of friends there. I've listen many stories in those two years. Seem someone cry sadly, some laught loudly. I thought that "Everyone are uniqe, every life journey are wonderful".
After closing my store, I thought I can't wast those life stories, so I decided to made this show, and continue the action of sharing stories.

Research

Since last year Septemver, I've made over 40 episodes, and interviewed over 30 different jobs. It is important to create variety questions for each interviewees, so I must to research lots of informations for those jobs. Not only search the content of those jobs, the most primary key is to find out the issues of the jobs. In each interview, I'll wrote down over 15 qustions, and highlight those issues that interested me.

The second type to have a good talk, is try not to ask questions by asking. It'll be better for me to set questions in conversations. It can guide the visitors to answer in more a interesting way.
