Concept

This is a documentary of Taiwan. The vedio display the beauty of Taiwan by 5 minutes."That everyone love Taiwan" is our main idea of this project. Landscape are no the only breath taking part of Taiwan, and also have lots of amazing culture in this small island.

Script

This is a project with my friends, we spent 1 mounth to finish this project. We planned to visit all around Taiwan and film it. Use quick cut on editing to preview the beauty of my Country.
We have 8 team members with different skills. So we divide the work for each phase of the project. I responsible for the film and editing.

Film

I'm a charge of the second camera on this project, film the close up and side shot are the work of mine.
To control the stabilizer is the most difficult part of all my film work, because I must hold the stabilizer for a long time, and switch the lens on speed.

Edit

I handle the style of the editing. While we were on the filming travel, I edited few versions with 1 minute for others to choose. This is an important phase to nail down the style for this hole vedio.
Ather all the filming,we oraganize all the file. We filmed more then 2TB at this project. Pick the film with dirctor and other team members.
Then I start to edit the longer vision for another editer to continue the project.
