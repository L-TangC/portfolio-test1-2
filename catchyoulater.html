Concept

This project is the graduation produciton of mine. It's an animate that cooperate with 2D and 3D.
The issue of the story is about “Dementia". The story describe a old man have a advanture at home. He think his still a young boy and having advature around different places.

Ideate

My grandmother is the inspiration for this story. She didn't have the Dementia, but have the simlar problems as other old perssn. At the time, my grandmother was about 95 years old. Sometimes she'll confused the momory. For example, she'll look at me and call my father's name. Even told me to call somebody I never met before, maybe her old friends.

Design

"Cooperate 2D and 3D animate" is the main idea of this project. We decided to draw 2D charcter with 3D Scenarios. We tried lots of methods to solve the difficulties on the 3D tool's texture. So we decrease the counts of mesh and add stroke for every 3D items. This method's benefit not only can render faster, also can draw details on UV.

Character

The charcters that we create are all around the protanonist. The old man and the child are the same person.The kid is the symbol of the old man's memerey and advanturous.
His son have be seen as a baboon at the end on the stoy. Last of the, the monster is the cockroach from the toilet.

Storyboard

To create a story includes the realistic active and inside the dream. I take reference with a famous animation director Kon Sotoshi. He use lots of special transition effects and type to present between real and dreams.
I pay tribute Kon Sotoshi movie on the back part of my animate. Not only make one continuous shot,also draw multiple transition effects, like connect two take by eyeball and muzzle, to rich my project.
