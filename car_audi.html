Concept

This project is a second hand car commercial film. Vedio displays the funtions of the car, Audi A8.

Proposal

Client told me that we want to have their own logo motion. Have "High class" and "Useful" style in the video. The other task for this project is to make a tempelate for client that could film and edit by themselves at the future.

Film

Before the filming, teammate and I have disscus all the images and lens that we must to film. Chick the funtions that the clients want to show. At the filming day, we set lots of cameras to convenient us to film. We only takes 1 hour on film works.

Edit

It's easy to edit the project by having the script. Let the video looks more "High class" and "Useful", I use the different tempo at the head, middle and the end of the video.
At the start and end of the video, I made motions for the information phase and the logo. I use multiple effect sources to made the logo motions. Then I reset the color and the keyframes of the sources to create an uniqe items.
