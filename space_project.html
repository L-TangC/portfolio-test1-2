Concept

"Space" is a vacuum packing machine for users to storage their clothes. This product is also a fundraising project. It's convenient for users to stoarge not only at home, also it's good for users to use it at the travels.

Proposal

We wrote the story about a couple use the product to save their relationship.
They solve lots of product by using the Space. Not only at home, but also using while they were camping and traveling.
